It has been largely recognized that vegetarian is one of the cheapest ways to eat sustainably (think rice and beans). A few things to note about that number: </p><ul id="203e37a8-402b-4b00-9e12-331223dcaae4" class="bulleted-list"><li>I am excluding groceries that I spent on my birthday party.
